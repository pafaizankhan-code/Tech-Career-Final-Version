01 / About · Mission · Vision
About the Studio

Built on craft, not hype.

Tech Career IT Solutions LLP was founded in 2025, out of a simple frustration - that small and mid-sized Indian businesses kept paying for websites and software that looked fine in a demo but fell apart two months after handover.

So we set up a small, founder-led studio at iHub, Ahmedabad - focused on the work we love doing: clean websites, reliable e-commerce stores, custom CRM and HRMS tools, mobile apps, and the digital marketing that actually brings traffic in. We are new as a company, but we are not new to the craft.

Mission & Vision
- Mission

Make good software feel within reach.

Give small and mid-sized Indian businesses the kind of websites, stores and internal tools that usually only big-budget teams get - at a price and pace that actually fits their business.

- Vision

Grow slowly. Build things that last.

Grow one happy client at a time - and become the studio that businesses in Ahmedabad and across India recommend to the next business, without us ever asking.

We do not have ten years of case studies yet. What we have is a small team, a clear focus, and the patience to earn every client the slow way - by doing the work well.
